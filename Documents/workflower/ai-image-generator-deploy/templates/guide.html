Everything you need to know to create amazing AI-generated images

Welcome to Unlimagen! Creating AI-generated images is easy and fun. Follow this guide to get the best results from our AI image generator.

✍️ Writing Effective Prompts

The prompt is your description of what you want to create. Good prompts lead to better results!

Basic Prompt Structure:

  1. Subject: What is the main focus? (e.g., "mountain", "person", "dragon")
  2. Details: Add specific characteristics (colors, size, mood)
  3. Style: Specify the art style (photorealistic, cartoon, painting)
  4. Quality: Add quality keywords (detailed, high quality, professional)

Examples of Good Prompts:

  • Bad: "a cat"
  • Good: "cute orange cat sitting on a windowsill, soft lighting, photorealistic, detailed fur"
  • Bad: "landscape"
  • Good: "mountain landscape at sunset, dramatic clouds, golden hour lighting, photorealistic, 8k quality"
💡 Pro Tip: Use descriptive adjectives! Words like "majestic", "vibrant", "serene", "dramatic" help the AI understand the mood you want.

⚙️ Choosing the Right Settings

AI Models:

We offer three different AI models, each with unique strengths:

  • ✨ Miragic AI: Best for high-quality, detailed images. Great for photorealistic results.
  • ⚡ Turbo AI: Fastest generation. Perfect when you need quick results.
  • 🎨 Creative AI: Most artistic and creative interpretations. Best for artistic styles.

Image Type:

  • Photorealistic: Real-looking photos
  • Digital Art: Modern artistic style
  • 3D Render: Three-dimensional appearance
  • Illustration: Hand-drawn look
  • Anime: Japanese animation style

Aspect Ratio:

  • Square (1:1): Perfect for social media posts, profile pictures
  • Landscape (16:9): Great for wallpapers, presentations, YouTube thumbnails
  • Portrait (9:16): Ideal for phone wallpapers, Instagram stories
💡 Pro Tip: Choose your aspect ratio based on where you'll use the image. Square works universally, but specific ratios look better in their intended context.

🎓 Advanced Techniques

Adding Style Keywords:

Enhance your prompts with these powerful keywords:

  • Quality: "8k", "4k", "high resolution", "detailed", "sharp"
  • Lighting: "soft lighting", "dramatic lighting", "golden hour", "neon lights"
  • Camera: "close-up", "wide angle", "aerial view", "bokeh effect"
  • Mood: "peaceful", "epic", "mysterious", "cheerful", "dark"
  • Art Style: "oil painting", "watercolor", "digital art", "sketch"

Combining Concepts:

Create unique images by combining different ideas:

  • "Cyberpunk city meets medieval castle"
  • "Cute cat dressed as a samurai warrior"
  • "Nature landscape in anime art style"
💡 Pro Tip: Don't be afraid to be creative! The AI can combine concepts in surprising and beautiful ways.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Too Vague: "something cool" → Instead: "futuristic sports car with neon lights"
  • Too Complex: Avoid prompts with 20+ different elements
  • Conflicting Styles: Don't mix "photorealistic" with "cartoon style" in one prompt
  • No Quality Keywords: Always add "high quality" or "detailed" for best results
  • Wrong Aspect Ratio: Choose the ratio that fits your intended use
