Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Unlimagen and our AI image generation service.

What is Unlimagen?
Unlimagen is a free AI-powered image generator that creates images from text descriptions. Simply describe what you want to see, and our AI will generate a unique image for you instantly.
Is Unlimagen really free?
Yes! Unlimagen is 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium plans. You can generate unlimited images without any cost.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is required. You can start generating images immediately without signing up or providing any personal information.
How do I generate an image?
Simply type a description of the image you want in the text box, select your preferred AI model and settings, then click "Generate Image". The AI will create your image in seconds.
What kind of images can I create?
You can create various types of images including 3D renders, photorealistic images, digital art, illustrations, anime-style art, abstract art, paintings, and minimalist designs.
Can I download the generated images?
Yes! Once your image is generated, you can download it directly to your device by clicking the "Download Image" button.
Can I use the generated images commercially?
The images are generated for your use. However, AI-generated content may have limitations on copyright protection depending on your jurisdiction. Please review our Terms of Service for more details.
How long does it take to generate an image?
Most images are generated within a few seconds, depending on the AI model you choose and server load. Our Speed Model is the fastest option.
What are the different AI models?
We offer three AI models: Quality Model (best quality with enhancement), Balanced Model (good balance of speed and quality), and Speed Model (fastest generation time).
Are there any limits on image generation?
No! You can generate unlimited images. There are no daily limits or restrictions.
Do you store my images?
No. We do not permanently store your generated images or text prompts. Images are generated in real-time and displayed directly in your browser.
What if my image doesn't look right?
Try rephrasing your description, being more specific, or switching to a different AI model. Different prompts and settings can produce very different results.
Can I generate inappropriate content?
No. We prohibit the generation of illegal, harmful, violent, hateful, or sexually explicit content. Please use our service responsibly and review our Terms of Service.
Is Unlimagen available on mobile?
Yes! Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.
How can I contact support?
If you have questions or issues, please visit our Contact page.
