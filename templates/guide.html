‚úçÔ∏è Writing Effective Prompts

The prompt is your description of what you want to create. Good prompts lead to better results!

Basic Prompt Structure:

  1. Subject: What is the main focus? (e.g., "mountain", "person", "dragon")
  2. Details: Add specific characteristics (colors, size, mood)
  3. Style: Specify the art style (photorealistic, cartoon, painting)
  4. Quality: Add quality keywords (detailed, high quality, professional)

Examples of Good Prompts:

  • Bad: "a cat"
  • Good: "cute orange cat sitting on a windowsill, soft lighting, photorealistic, detailed fur"
  • Bad: "landscape"
  • Good: "mountain landscape at sunset, dramatic clouds, golden hour lighting, photorealistic, detailed"
üí° Pro Tip: Use descriptive adjectives! Words like "majestic", "vibrant", "serene", "dramatic" help the AI understand the mood you want.

üéì Advanced Techniques

Adding Style Keywords:

Enhance your prompts with these powerful keywords:

  • Quality: "detailed", "sharp", "clear", "well-defined"
  • Lighting: "soft lighting", "dramatic lighting", "golden hour", "neon lights"
  • Camera: "close-up", "wide angle", "aerial view", "bokeh effect"
  • Mood: "peaceful", "epic", "mysterious", "cheerful", "dark"
  • Art Style: "oil painting", "watercolor", "digital art", "sketch"

Combining Concepts:

Create unique images by combining different ideas:

  • "Cyberpunk city meets medieval castle"
  • "Cute cat dressed as a samurai warrior"
  • "Nature landscape in anime art style"
üí° Pro Tip: Don't be afraid to be creative! The AI can combine concepts in surprising and beautiful ways.
